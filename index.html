About this initiative

Started by VNIT alumni.
Built for every junior.

We graduated from NIT Nagpur in 2025 and looked back at our own JEE journey. The thing that made the real difference wasn't the resources — it was that one senior who said "bhai ye mat karo, ye karo."

Most 11th and 12th students preparing for JEE never get that conversation. They have material but no clarity, effort but no direction. We built MentorBridge to change that.

We're starting small, staying personal, and making sure every student gets a mentor who's actually been through their exact situation.

"The best guidance comes from someone who remembers what it felt like to not know the answer."

Who we are

VNIT Nagpur '25 alumni — engineers who remember the JEE journey and want to make it easier for the next batch.

Who this is for

Class 11 and Class 12 students preparing for JEE Main and Advanced. Any target — IIT, NIT, or GFTI.

Our mentors

Only verified IIT and NIT students and alumni. Every mentor is someone we personally know and trust.

Our promise

Every form is personally reviewed. You'll hear back within 3 days. First session within a week of matching.
